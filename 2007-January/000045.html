the BerliOS website, and thus also the fetchmail.info website, has been
hard to reach because it has been under a distributed denial of service
attack.  Details at
<https://developer.berlios.de/forum/forum.php?forum_id=23804>

Downloading fetchmail is also possible via via ibiblio.org/pub/Linux and
its mirror sites, in the system/mail/pop/fetchmail/ directory.
Their - somewhat outdated - mirror list is at
http://ibiblio.org/pub/linux/MIRRORS.html
ftp://ftp.gwdg.de/pub/linux/mirrors/sunsite/MIRRORS.html
ftp://ftp.oss.cc.gatech.edu/pub/linux/MIRRORS.html
